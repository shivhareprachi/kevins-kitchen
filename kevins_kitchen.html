Leafy Green Salad with Fresh Figs, Beets and Avocado

I have made this recipe to my taste, with the tang-levels that suit me (and my loved ones, it seems) but please feel free to adjust to suit your taste – for example more or less lemon juice, less garlic or even more if you’re so inclined. Taste along the way and enjoy.

Quantities: Serves 4 Timings: 30 mins Kitchen Stuff: Large non-stick frying pan, whisk, strong knife, baking tray, griddle pan

INGREDIENTS: 2 large chicken breasts 5 smoked bacon rashers 1/2 cup parmesan shavings (using a cheese scraper, scrape off swirls or lengths from the parmesan) 2-3 large slices of day old sourdough or crusty bloomer (ciabatta also works well) Olive oil for drizzling 1/2 tsp garlic granules (optional) 15g butter (1 heaped tsp) 1 bag rocket leaves (75g) 2 romaine lettuce hearts Sea salt & freshly ground black pepper

Cucumber Tomato Avocado Salad

Our family’s favorite classic cucumber and tomato salad just got better with the addition of avocado, a light and flavorful lemon dressing and the freshness of cilantro. Easy, excellent avocado salad.

INGREDIENTS:This salad is loaded with veggies; juicy tomatoes, crisp cucumber, creamy avocado and the cilantro and lemon juice make the whole salad so fresh and flavorful!

1. Place chopped tomatoes, sliced cucumber, sliced red onion, diced avocado, and chopped cilantro into a large salad bowl.

2. Drizzle with 2 Tbsp olive oil and 2 Tbsp lemon juice. Toss gently to combine. Just before serving toss with 1 tsp sea salt and 1/8 tsp black pepper

1 lb Roma tomatoes, 1 English cucumber, 1/2 medium red onion sliced, 2 avocados diced, 2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil or sunflower oil, 2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice (from 1 medium lemon), 1/4 cup 1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped, 1 tsp sea salt or 3/4 tsp table salt, 1/8 tsp black pepper.
