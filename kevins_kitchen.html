    CAESAR SALAD

    • Vegetable Hakka Noodles

    ×
    Ingredients:

    1 tbsp olive oil,1 tbsp Ginger, chopped,1 tbsp Garlic, chopped,1 tbsp Celery, chopped,1 medium onion sliced,Salt to taste,1 cup carrot julienne,1 cup French beans , sliced diagonally,1 tsp chili sauce,1 tsp sugar.¼ cup vegetable stock,½ tsp crushed black pepper

    Preparation

    Heat oil in a wok or a non-stick kadhai, add ginger, garlic and celery. Saute for a couple of minutes until light golden in colour. Add the sliced onions, salt and sauté. Let the onion sweat and become soft. Now add the chopped vegetables (carrot, cabbage, beans) and sauté. Add the chili sauce, soy sauce, sugar and little stock or water. Mix well, cover and cook for a few minutes. When the vegetables are half done (do not over cook) add the boiled noodles and toss. Mix the noodles well with rest of the ingredients. Season with salt and pepper. Add the spring onion and mix well. Serve hot. Category: Veg Non Veg Vegan Special Recipes Cuisines Health Special Ingredient
